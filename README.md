# Quantitative Finance & Stochastic Systems Portfolio

This repository serves as a structured collection of quantitative finance models, focusing on the intersection of advanced mathematics, stochastic calculus, and systems engineering. 

The primary objective is to translate complex financial theories into robust, computable architectures. By approaching quantitative finance through an engineering lens, the models prioritize modularity, numerical stability, and logical system design.

## Repository Structure

### 📁 [Option-Pricing](./Option-Pricing/)
Implementations of continuous-time derivative pricing models and volatility dynamics.
* **Black-Scholes Framework:** Analytical closed-form solutions for European options.
* **Heston Model:** Simulation of coupled Stochastic Differential Equations (SDEs) for mean-reverting stochastic volatility.

*(Further directories covering Risk Management, Algorithmic Trading, and Time-Series Analysis will be added as part of ongoing research and development).*

## Technologies & Methodologies
* **Simulation & Modeling:** MATLAB, Simulink (Fixed-Step Discrete Solvers, Euler-Maruyama discretization).
* **Core Mathematics:** Stochastic Calculus, Econometrics, Probability Theory, Numerical Optimization.
